## 1. Project Identity

### The Perspective
**FMCG Whitespace OS** is a strategic PM portfolio asset designed to showcase systems-minded product management in the consumer goods sector. It represents a commitment to identifying massive whitespaces in legacy FMCG segments—cooler, newer, repositioned products built from real-world street signals rather than boardroom assumptions.

### The Objective
To demonstrate mastery in identifying and solving **The Optimization Problem** of "Better-for-you" products. The goal is to provide **Functional Control** in guilt-heavy zones (e.g., portion control, 100-calorie limits) where taste remains paramount and unit economics remain viable.

### The Tone: "The Strategic PM"
- **Systems-Minded**: Mapping the math of food tech to the realities of quick commerce.
- **Precision & Punch**: "Attention is a rare gift. Pack everything into that one punch."
- **Data-Backed**: Rooted in category research, consumer behavior, and regulatory realities.
- **Zero Fluff**: Professional, direct, and focused on the value proposition.

## 2. Core Pillars & Philosophy

1.  **Whitespaces Everywhere**: Legacy FMCG is slow. Newer, repositioned products can win by simply looking where others refuse to look.
2.  **Functional Indulgence**: Health is niche; Indulgence is mass. We balance the "Sine Curve" by building health into indulgent formats.
3.  **The Math of Taste**: "Better-for-you" is an optimization problem—removing guilt-heavy ingredients without losing the sensory "Love and Like" bracket.
4.  **Damage Control Approach**: Reducing the "damage" of modern eating patterns without requiring a complete lifestyle overhaul.
5.  **Unit Economics First**: Strategy is irrelevant if the LTV:CAC math doesn't work in the Indian price-sensitive market.

## 3. Project Architecture (4 Core Universes)

1.  **Home (`/`)**: **The Atmospheric Entry.** Establishes the profile of a systems-minded builder. Uses cinematic video backgrounds to set a premium, editorial stage.
